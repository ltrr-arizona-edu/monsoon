Tree-Ring Monsoon Workshops

Since 2011, our research group has hosted workshops on tree ring records of monsoon variability in both Arizona and New Mexico. These half day workshops, based on the format of past TreeFlow workshops for water managers, were geared towards natural resource managers and other monsoon stakeholders. Our group is making preparation for future workshops. For information about upcoming events, please email Connie Woodhouse.
